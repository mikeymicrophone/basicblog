jScrollPane text size change example

This demo shows how to use jScrollPane alongside jQEm so that your scroll bar resizes and updates as the user resizes the text on your page using the browser's controls.

It's very simple - just include the jQEm script in your page and jScrollPane does the rest! Resize the text in your browser to see it working...
